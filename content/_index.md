I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [Techno-Anthropology Lab](https://www.en.culture.aau.dk/research/research-groups/tantlab), [Aalborg University](https://vbn.aau.dk/en/persons/alexanderg/), working on algorithmic markets and AI governance. My [research](/research/) examines how algorithmic systems are designed, adopted, and governed in insurance markets, in professional work, and across the public sector, drawing on economic sociology, science and technology studies, and the sociology of professions.
