I am a research assistant at the Department of Organization at Copenhagen Business School. My research is primarily focused on professionals and algorithmic work in the insurance industry. I am fully funded by the Algorithms,Data & Democracy project. See more in my CV.

In my spare time, I write, teach and talk about subjective wellbeing, happiness and the Wellbeing Economy. This is a remainder of my prior work with my wonderful and talented collegaues at the Happiness Research Institute, a Copenhagen based think tank.

Analytically, I am interested in statistics and relational methods such as social network analysis. I enjoy coding in R. Some of this work, I am sharing on my blog.
