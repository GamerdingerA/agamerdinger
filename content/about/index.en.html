I am a PhD Fellow at the Department of Organization at Copenhagen Business School. I have a BSc and MSc in International Business and Politics from CBS specializing in International Political Economy and wellbeing indicators.

After my education, I started a PhD fellowship at CBS where I research the use of data and new technologies in insurance. Theoretically, I draw on Sociology of Professions and Sociology of Insurance. My research is part of the Algorithms,Data & Democracy project which includes several university in Denmark.

In my spare time, I am passionate about coding in R, following the wellbeing and post-growth movement, and swimming.

If you would like to know more about me, check out my CV, blog or send me an e-mail at aga.ioa@cbs.dk
