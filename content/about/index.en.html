I am a PhD Fellow at the Department of Organization at Copenhagen Business School. My research is primarily focused on professionals and algorithmic work in the insurance industry. I am fully funded by the Algorithms,Data & Democracy project.

If you want to know more, check out my CV

In my spare time, I write, teach and talk about subjective wellbeing, happiness and the Wellbeing Economy. This is a remainder of my prior work with my talented colleagues at the Happiness Research Institute, a Copenhagen based Think Tank.

I enjoy working with quantitative data, using statistical methods, social network analysis and text analysis in R.
