I am a PhD Fellow at the Department of Organization at Copenhagen Business School. While I have a background in International Political Economy, my research is situated in Economic Sociology, Sociology of Professions and Sociology of Insurance. My research is part of the Algorithms,Data & Democracy consortium, which includes several danish universities and the think-tank Mandag Morgen.

In my spare time, I am passionate about coding in R, following the wellbeing and post-growth movement, and swimming.

If you would like to know more about me, check out my CV, blog or send me an e-mail at aga.ioa@cbs.dk
